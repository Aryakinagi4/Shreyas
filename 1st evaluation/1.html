SMART INDIA HACKATHON

Let us understand what Smart India Hackathon is !

Smart India Hackathon is an all over india event where the competitions across all the domains like machine learning, Web development, Computer Networks, Web semantics etc take place. Students from different Colleges across India come and show off their skills, learn new things and win different prizes. It is a great opportunity for many students to build their profile to maintain their standard in today's world. So don't wait go and register !
